FAQ

FAQ

Will the course be suitable for me
if I can't draw at all?

+

Yes. The course is suitable for both beginners and those who already know how to draw but want to elevate their skills. However, it's important to understand that we expect dedicated effort on assignments from you — the pace will be dynamic, no fluff, just practice, a lot of practice.

Will I be able to view video lessons
in my personal account without internet access?

+

Unfortunately, you won't be able to view the lesson without internet access since all classes are located in your personal account on the website.

What age restrictions
does the training program have?

+

The courses do not have age restrictions; you can learn to draw at any age.

What if I don't like
the course?

+

If after the first class you realize that the course is not suitable for you, just let us know, and we'll refund your money.

Is my access to the course forever?

+

We provide access to the courses for 6 months so that you can review the lessons multiple times.

How long does a lesson last
and how much time is needed
for its completion?

+

On average, the duration of a lesson is 30-90 minutes. The completion time of homework is individual and depends on the student's initial abilities.

How will I get access to the Telegram chat
with teachers and students?

+

After purchasing the course, you will receive access to your personal account, as well as links and instructions on joining the closed chat with all students and the teacher.

I'm from another country/city,
can I still study with you?

+

Yes, our online courses are designed for students worldwide. We also plan to launch courses in English.

Is it possible to study from a mobile phone?

+

Yes, the personal account has a mobile version, so you can comfortably study even from your phone.

What will I need to complete the assignments?

+

For each course, we have prepared a detailed list of materials that we recommend purchasing if you don't have them yet.

Do you want to purchase a gift certificate?

+

If you want to give our course as a gift to a friend or loved one, we will create a beautiful gift certificate for you. It can be personalized for an even more pleasant gift. The certificate will be ready within one working day from the order, and then we will send it in high quality to the email you provided.

To order a certificate, write or call us, and the administrator will answer all your questions and quickly arrange everything.
