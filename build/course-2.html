Living Characters- What is it?

Living
Characters- What is it?

Living characters are a stylization and caricature of a particular image. Every day, you encounter incredible characters in cartoons, books, video games, and advertisements. They live in their fairy-tale worlds and parallel universes. If you love fantastic worlds, characters, and have a desire to draw your own heroes but lack experience, then this course is designed just for you!

Программаобучения

Программа курса включает в себя 8 занятий, на которых ты научишься
создавать уникальных и неповторимых героев.

Программа курса
1

Lesson: Understanding the Proportions of the Human Figure.

We will start the first lesson by familiarizing ourselves with the basic proportions of the human figure. We will study the important features of male and female figures. Explore various body types, and by simplifying them into understandable and simple forms, we will embark on our journey of creating characters.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

2

Lesson: Dynamic and Static Poses.

In the second lesson, we will sketch various human poses and study the dynamics of human body movement. You will learn how the line of motion helps create a character in both static and dynamic situations.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

3

Lesson: Basic Proportions of the Human Head.

In the third lesson, we will examine the basic proportions of the face and sketch various perspectives of the head. Based on the studied proportions, we will explore and sketch different face types.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

4

Lesson: Emotions.

In this lesson, we will explore the palette of character emotions. Drawing on the knowledge gained from this lesson and the previous ones, we will draw different faces and characters with various emotions.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

5

Lesson: Character Development in Different Styles.

In the fifth lesson, we will draw characters in different cartoon styles to understand how stylistic features can be used to play with the character's image.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

6

Lesson: Character Lineup.

In this lesson, we will develop a lineup of characters, examining how age, gender, nationality, and other factors influence the character's personality.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

7

Lesson: Working on Clothing and Accessories.

In the seventh lesson, we will move on to a more detailed development of characters. Based on characters from a series, we will draw characters in a specific era. We will understand how the environment, class affiliation, and profession influence the character's image and personality.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

8

Lesson: Character in the Environment.

The character's environment is crucial in their development. In the final class of the course, we will create a character within the context of its environment. We will draw a full-color illustration.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework
