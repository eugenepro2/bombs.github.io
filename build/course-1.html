Architectural
Sketching - What is it?

Architectural (urban) sketching is one of the most
modern and popular trends in drawing
today. Its main advantage
is that the sketch is done
at a fairly quick pace and allows you to convey your idea
and concept in a short
period of time!

Training Program

The course program consists of 8 sessions, each of which
we will be creating a new architectural sketch.

Course Program
1

Session: Pencil Technique

In the first session, we will explore the laws of perspective and, using cube construction as an example, learn to apply them. Then, we will create a linear drawing of a building in frontal perspective and understand how to use pencil strokes correctly.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

2

Session: Working with Gel Pen

In this session, we will sketch a more complex architectural structure, learn to depict angular perspective, and create cross-hatching with a gel pen.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

3

Session: Working with Color Pencils (1-3)

Using the techniques and knowledge from the previous lesson, we will create an architectural composition at the edge of the sheet and learn to emphasize the image with colored pencils.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

4

Session: Linework with Watercolor

In this session, we will create the structure and detail an architectural building. Then, we will introduce watercolors and learn to create subtle color blends.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

5

Session: Dry Brush Technique (Black Gouache)

In this lesson, we will master another new technique, learn to use a brush with paint as the main drawing tool, and explore techniques that will make the image contrasting and vibrant.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

6

Session: Watercolor Technique

In this session, we will delve deeper into watercolors and their properties. We will learn to mix colors and create a multicolored image.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

7

Session: Quick Sketch with Liner Without Construction

In this lesson, we will set aside simple pencils and create a liner sketch without preliminary marking and construction. The ability to perform a quick "freehand" drawing is the essence of architectural sketching.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework

8

Session: Final Course Project

In the final session, we will complete the graduation project using the dry brush technique and get acquainted with another interesting drawing tool, the stump.

Duration: From 35-90 mins. + homework
